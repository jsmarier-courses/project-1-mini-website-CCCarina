Gatineau Park is the conservation park of the National Capital Region. The park's rich biodiversity and proximity to downtown make it an ideal spot to enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, cycling, canoeing, and skiing. Besides, many recreational infrastructures such as beaches, campgrounds, picnic areas, trails, and parkways are also available at Gatineau Park. Therefore, Gatineau Park can attract thousands of visitors each year and become the second-most visited park in Canada.
